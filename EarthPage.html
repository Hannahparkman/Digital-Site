Earth

  1. Polar Diameter: 12,714 km
  2. Equatorial Diameter: 12,756 km
  3. Mass: 5.97 x 10^24 kg

Facts

  1. On average the Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System
  2. It is the only planet that has liquid water on its surface
  3. From space much of the Earth’s surface cannot be seen due to the clouds of water vapour
  4. The earth gravitates around the sun
  5. A year on Earth isn’t 365 days it is 365.2564 days, the extra .2564 creates the need for a Leap Year
