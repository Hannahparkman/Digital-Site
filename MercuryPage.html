Mercury

  1. Diameter: 4,879 km
  2. Mass: 3.30 x 10^23 kg

Facts

  1. Mercury the smallest planet in our solar system
  2. Your weight on Mercury is 38% of your weight on Earth
  3. A year on Mercury is 88 Earth days
  4. Mercury has wrinkles which are called Lobate Scarps
  5. After Earth, Mercury is the second densest planet
