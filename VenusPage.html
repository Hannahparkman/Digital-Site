Venus

  1. Diameter: 12,104 km
  2. Mass: 4.87x 10^24 kg

Facts

  1. Venus doesn’t have any moons or rings
  2. It rotates in the opposite direction to the Sun, the rest of the planets, except Uranus rotate anticlockwise
  3. The temperature of the surface on Venus can reach 471 °C
  4. Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system
  5. Venus is the second brightest object in the night sky, after the Moon
